AUTOMATION AND THE INDUSTRY

This project was an animated documentary originally created for the CSPAN StudentCam 2020. It was modified for submission into the Sacramento Educational Video Awards and the Northern California National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences Student Production Awards. Automation and The Industry placed 1st in both events, and is currently competiting for a national ranking in the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.
